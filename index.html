EID-UL-ADHA
Islamic Festival
Eid al-Fitr is a significant Islamic festival that marks the end of Ramadan, the month of fasting. It is a day of joy, gratitude, and celebration with family and friends.
CHRISTMAS
Christian Festival
Christmas celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ and is a time for love, sharing, and spreading goodwill. It is marked by festive decorations, family gatherings, and exchanging gifts.
VESAK
Buddhist Festival
Vesak celebrates the birth, enlightenment, and death of the Buddha. It is a time for Buddhists to reflect on the teachings of the Buddha and engage in acts of kindness and charity.
DIWALI
Hindu Festival
Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is celebrated by Hindus to mark the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It involves lighting lamps, feasting, and spending time with family.
